Up!


Carl Fredrickson who is the main character of the movie Up! Is a 78 year old who's about to fulfill a promise he made to his late wife, Ellie Fredrickson. The promise is to move their house to paradise falls. Charles Muntz is the person who inspired both Carl and Ellie to adventure to the paradise falls. With the use of thousands of balloons he was able to set to the air towards his destination. However a boy scout he was not fond of named Russel was onboard on the flying house he was in, starting they're journey.
